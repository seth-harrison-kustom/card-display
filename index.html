⚠ Acrylic Warning
Do NOT buy UV-filtering or "UV-stabilized" acrylic. Most sign shops default to this. Specify standard clear cast acrylic only — the UV light must pass through it.
🪚 Dado Tip
Cut all your shelf dado grooves before assembly. A ¼" straight router bit or table saw dado stack both work. Test with a scrap toploader before final cuts.
💡 LED Placement
Mount the UV strip along the inside top rail facing down. Add a second strip along the bottom rail facing up if you want more punch. Top-only is usually enough.
🃏 Card Protection
UV light on for short viewing sessions only — not continuous display lighting. The on/off switch is the key safety feature. Cards in toploaders get extra protection.
🎨 Finish Options
Flat black paint on MDF looks sharp and lets the UV glow pop. Hardwood with dark walnut stain is a step up. Avoid glossy finishes inside — they reflect and wash out the UV effect.
